Sugo di pomodoro

Delicious with meatballs and pasta

Disclaimer: this recipe will result in a focaccia

Ingredients

  • Flour, 500g
  • Water, 300g
  • Yeast, 5g
  • EVO oil, 20g
  • Salt, 15g

Steps

  1. In a bowl, add the flour and yeast, mix to break the yeast
  2. Add water a little at a time and mix well
  3. When the dough is smooth, add oil and salt
  4. Make it rest under a bowl for 4 hours
  5. Cook in oven at 180 C for 30 minutes.
