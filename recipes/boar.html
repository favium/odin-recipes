Boar: the piggy that ran too slow

Who doesn't like some good meat?
Vikings were no exception, and Odin surely loved to eat some roasted piggy!


Ingredients

Please note: this recipe contains some physical activity.

  • 1 pig
  • 1 fire

Preparation

  1. Run after a boar, catch it, and kill it
  2. Start the fire
  3. Put pig on fire
  4. Wait 1 hour
  5. Enjoy!
