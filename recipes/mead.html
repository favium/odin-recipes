Mead: the Gods' Golden Drink


Mead has been known for ages as the Gods' Golden drink.
One of the first alcoholic beverages known to man, Odin went absolutely bonkers for it! Here's how to prepare it:

Ingredients

For some really sweet mead:

  • 1L water
  • 200g honey
  • 15g wine yeast

Preparation

  1. Warm up the water
  2. Insert the honey and mix well
  3. When honey is dissolved and the solution cooled down slightly, insert the yeast
  4. Put the solution in a jar and wait 3 or 4 months
  5. Filter and enjoy!
