Don't we all love waging war to our neighbors? Here's how it's done, in case you're such a bad Viking you forgot...

Ingredients

You'll really need the following:

  • A group of able-bodied men
  • Someone you really don't like
  • Optional: Weapons

Preparation

  1. Get your men
  2. Find enemy
  3. Smash, hit, slash, punch
  4. Win (optional)
  5. Go home
Bonus tip: try not to get hit!
